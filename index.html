Short Biography

Manuel Boldrer received the master's degree in Mechatronic Engineering and Ph.D degree in Materials, Mechatronics and Systems Engineering from the University of Trento, Trento, Italy, respectively in 2018 and 2022. He was a Visiting Scholar at the University of California, Riverside, Riverside, US, in 2021. As of today, he is a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Cognitive Robotics Department (CoR), Delft University of Technology, Delft, Netherlands, in the Reliable Robot Control Lab.
